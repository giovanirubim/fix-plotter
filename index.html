Input data

You can edit the input data above to insert your own data.

A line that starts with AP: followed by a pair of coordinates sets your assumed position.

A line that starts with - Name: starts the description of a new sight, and should be followed by the name of the celestial body sighted .

Ho: followed by an angle, sets the observed altitude of the current sight.

Dec: followed by an angle, sets the declination of the celestial body of the current sight.

GHA: followed by an angle, sets the GHA of the celestial body of the current sight.

Sights will automatically be turned into lines of position. Once two or three lines of positions are present, a fix will be done.
